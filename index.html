Dutch Categories And Types Seminar

The Dutch Categories And Types Seminar is an inter-university seminar on type theory, category theory, and the interaction between these two fields. It provides a forum for discussion, collaboration, and dissemination to researchers in type theory and category working in the Netherlands.

Inaugural Meeting: Amsterdam, 27 October 2021
