City

Leiden is reachable from Amsterdam Central in 35 minutes by train; there is also a direct train from Schiphol airport taking 15 minutes. The city is quite small, and the venue is located in the city center. Walking is a good way of getting around the center, with most places only 20 minutes of walking appart. In addition, there is the option of taking a bus, and it is possible to rent bikes.

Venue

We will gather in the Kamerlingh Onnes Building (room A0.51) of Leiden University.
